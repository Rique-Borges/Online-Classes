The Best Movies According to Rike

My top 5 movies of all time

Trainspotting

It's an incredible movie about the use an consequences of heroin addiction

Lilja-4-Ever

One of the most depressing movies ever recorded, it highlights the horrors of human trafficking

2001: A Space Odyssey

An Sci-Fi horror/epic that shows the struggles of humanity advancing to the next stage of evolution, and the horrors of artificial intelligences

Hot Fuzz

A cop movie parody starring Nick Frost and Simon Pegg, with Edgar Wright as the director. Need i say more?

Brokeback Mountain

A crushingly sad tale of two homosexual cowboys in 1963, that highlights how homophobia can deeply strain a gay relationship
